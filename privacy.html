Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Weeknight ("we", "us", or "our") operates the weeknight.food website and SMS meal planning service. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information.

Information We Collect

When you use our service, we collect:

  • Phone number: To send you meal plans and communicate via SMS
  • Meal preferences: Dietary restrictions, household size, and cooking preferences you share with us
  • Feedback: Your responses about meals and recipes
  • Payment information: Processed securely through Stripe; we do not store your credit card details

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Send you weekly meal plans and grocery lists
  • Personalize recommendations based on your preferences and feedback
  • Process payments for the service
  • Respond to your questions and requests
  • Improve our service

SMS/Text Messaging

By providing your phone number and opting in, you consent to receive text messages from Weeknight related to meal planning, including:

  • Weekly meal plan suggestions
  • Shopping list notifications
  • Feedback requests about past meals
  • Service-related updates

Message frequency varies based on your usage, typically 3-10 messages per week. Message and data rates may apply.

We will not share or sell your mobile phone number to third parties for marketing purposes.

You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: Such as Twilio (SMS), Stripe (payments), and grocery store APIs (to build your cart)
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Opt out of SMS messages at any time by texting STOP

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at hello@weeknight.app.
